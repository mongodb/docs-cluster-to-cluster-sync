Verify with Embedded Verifier
=============================

.. versionadded:: 1.8

   ``mongosync`` embeds a verifier to run a series of checks against the source
   and destination clusters to verify that the sync was successful.

About this Task
---------------

Compatibility
~~~~~~~~~~~~~

The embedded verifier is not available in ``mongosync`` 1.7 and earlier.

For alternative verification methods, see :ref:`c2c-verification`.
